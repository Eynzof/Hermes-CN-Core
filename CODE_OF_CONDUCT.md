# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple community standard: be respectful, assume good intent, and keep discussion focused on the work.

The following behavior is not acceptable in this repository:

- harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks;
- publishing another person's private information;
- repeated spam, trolling, or derailing technical discussions;
- knowingly submitting malicious code, credential exfiltration, or supply-chain attacks.

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or accounts that violate this standard. Participation in this project means agreeing to follow these rules.

For security-sensitive reports, use [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) instead of public issues.
